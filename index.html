Learn. Intern & Work In Tech

A 1-year training program on Web Development, Product Design & Digital Marketing that will give you access to world-class resources, facilitators, and mentors, while getting a 3 month paid internship and work placement at the end of the program.

What JAUIT is

JAUIT is a 1-year program aimed at training Nigerians in a hybrid model on Web Development, Product Design & Marketing. If youre looking at breaking into tech or switching careers then this program is for you.

The training program will last for 5 months, with a 1-month project phase, and 3 months paid internship after which students will be place a role in their various paths.
